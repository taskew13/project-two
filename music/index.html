The Influence of Music within my Life

In order to learn more about me, there's no way that I couldn't include the topic of music. Raised in the Deep South by black parents, Gospel, RnB, Disco, and Soul music became a part of my being from an early age. In addition to this, the babysitter who used to take care of my sister and I in elementary school listened only to Hard Rock and Metal music, adding another two genres to my musical pallette. As I grew older and older, my music taste varied and changed, however, one thing stayed consistent throughtout these phases: the effect music has always had on me. For years, I have always used music to calm my anxiety down, uplift my mood, make me cry, complete assignments to, and any other scenario you can think of in the world. It's been there for me when friends and family never were, it's helped me through some of the hardest times in my life, and I don't think I could continue living if I was never able to hear music ever again. I want to share this passion and love of music with you, so here are a few of my favorite songs that I listen to depending on my mood or activity. Enjoy! :)
